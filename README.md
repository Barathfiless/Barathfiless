## &nbsp; Who I Am

<img align="right" width="300" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/abhisheknaiidu/abhisheknaiidu/master/code.gif"/>

I'm **Barath M**, a CS & Business Systems graduate from **Bannari Amman Institute of Technology**, Sathyamangalam — building at the intersection of full-stack engineering and applied AI.

My work lives in two worlds. On the engineering side, I architect MERN-stack systems and RESTful backends with FastAPI that handle real load and real users. On the AI side, I build RAG pipelines, fine-tune LLMs with QLoRA, and ship NLP tooling that actually solves problems.

I care deeply about writing **clean, production-ready code** — not just prototypes. Every project I ship has measurable impact: faster queries, fewer manual steps, higher throughput.

Currently based in **Erode, Tamil Nadu** — open to freelance work, collaborations, and meaningful open-source contributions.

> *"Build fast · Learn faster · Ship real things."*
